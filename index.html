TRuss and Cable FEM tools

TR2DL and TR2D are finite element Matlab codes for the analysis of two-dimensional truss structures. TR2DL is used for linear elastic deformations and TR2D for problems with geometrical and material nonlinearities. TR3D is a three-dimensional truss program which allows the modelling and prestressing of cables.
